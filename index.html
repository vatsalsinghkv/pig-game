Rules

  • If you roll a 1, you score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • If you roll any other number, it is added to your recent score and the your turn continues.
  • If you chooses to "hold", your turn total is added to your score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • Who's score reaches 50, will win the game.
